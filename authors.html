About the Authors

Meet the team behind this project dedicated to raising awareness about natural disasters and environmental challenges.

A 16-year-old high school student with a deep passion for computer science and technology. Remy is dedicated to environmental conservation and believes in the power of awareness to combat climate change. Through this website, he aims to educate others about natural disasters and their connection to climate change.

A Junior at High Technology High School with expertise in software development and engineering. His goal is to raise awareness about global warming's impact on natural disasters.
