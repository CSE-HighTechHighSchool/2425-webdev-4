About Tornados

Definition: Tornados are violently rotating columns of air that form at the base of a thunderstorm and go to the ground. They are visible only if a condensation funnel is formed. These are made up of water droplets, dust, and debris.

Survival Tips

Before the storm: Create an emergency kit (food, water, medicines). Know your evacuation routes. Secure your home (shutters, bring in outdoor items).

During the storm: Stay indoors, away from windows. Follow evacuation orders if issued. Use radio/alerts for updates.

After the storm: Avoid floodwaters and damaged areas. Check for power lines or structural damage.
